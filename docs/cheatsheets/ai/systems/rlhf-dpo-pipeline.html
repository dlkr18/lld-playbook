RLHF & DPO Pipeline // Systems

Align LLMs with human preferences: SFT baseline, reward modeling, PPO/RLHF, DPO direct preference optimization. Know when alignment training beats prompt engineering.

SFTReward ModelDPOPPOAlignment

Alignment pipeline: base model → SFT on demonstrations → preference pair collection → DPO or RLHF (reward model + PPO) → aligned model → eval harness gate.

01 — What & Why

Base LLMs optimize next-token prediction, not helpfulness or safety. Alignment training steers behavior toward human preferences.

03 — Architecture

Base LM -> SFT -> Collect prefs (A/B human rank) -> DPO train -> Eval gate -> Deploy

04 — Deep Dive: SFT Baseline

10K–100K high-quality (prompt, response) pairs. Must precede DPO. Teaches format and domain vocabulary.

05 — Deep Dive: Reward Model

Reward model scores chosen response higher than rejected; Bradley-Terry loss

Train classifier on preference pairs. Used as PPO reward signal. Monitor reward hacking.

06 — Deep Dive: DPO

DPO loss directly increases log-prob of chosen vs rejected without separate reward model

DPO is default for most teams: simpler than PPO, no reward model, stable on 7B–70B with LoRA.

07 — Deep Dive: PPO / RLHF

PPO fine-tunes policy against RM with KL penalty to base model. OpenAI original InstructGPT path. Expensive and unstable — reserved for frontier labs.

08 — Deep Dive: Alignment Eval

Win-rate vs baseline on held-out prefs. Safety red-team set. Capability eval (don't degrade MMLU). MT-Bench / Arena Elo.

12 — Interview Q&A

RLHF vs DPO?
RLHF: train reward model + PPO — complex, unstable. DPO: optimize preferences directly — simpler, default for product teams with LoRA.
What data for DPO?
Triplets: prompt, chosen response, rejected response. 5K–50K pairs. Diverse prompts; consistent ranking criteria.
Why SFT before DPO?
SFT teaches format and domain. DPO refines preferences on top. DPO alone on base model often fails to learn task format.
What is reward hacking?
Model optimizes RM score without being actually helpful — verbose, sycophantic answers. Mitigate with KL penalty and human eval.
PPO when still used?
Frontier labs with compute for online RL. Most enterprises use DPO or RLHF-DPO hybrid (Constitutional AI).
How eval alignment?
Preference win-rate on held-out set. Safety benchmarks. Regression on capability evals. Production thumbs up/down.
Alignment tax?
Alignment can slightly reduce raw reasoning scores. Monitor MMLU/GSM8K during training; early stop if >3% drop.
Human preference collection?
Side-by-side ranking UI. Multiple annotators. Inter-rater agreement >0.7. Balance easy/hard prompts.
DPO + LoRA?
Standard stack: QLoRA on 7B–70B with preference pairs. Train on 1–4 A100s for hours not weeks.
Constitutional AI?
AI generates critique/revision against principles. Reduces human label need. Used in Claude training pipeline.
Online vs offline RL?
Offline: DPO on fixed prefs. Online: PPO with live RM — higher risk, used at scale by OpenAI.
SDE3: alignment plan for domain assistant?
Month 1: SFT on 20K expert pairs. Month 2: collect 10K prefs from PM review. Month 3: DPO LoRA. Eval gate: win-rate >60% + no safety regression. Shadow deploy.
